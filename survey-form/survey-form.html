Crafting in the time of COVID-19

With all of the isolation brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic, many people turned to solitary activities such as craft-making in order to pass the time. This is a form designed to seek out information on what crafting hobbies people have, what projects they are currently working on, and how long they have been crafting.
