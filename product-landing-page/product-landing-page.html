About Us

Because you can never have too many dice…

Founded in 1987, Chessex® has had a long history of manufacturing gaming accessories with an emphasis on dice. Our goal is to provide high quality gaming accessories at an affordable price.

Chessex® strongly encourages consumers to buy from their local retail store whenever possible. Without distributors and retailers our industry would be quite different. Our success is due to the support and success of our hobby partners. We prefer that consumers buy anything they can locally or from their online retailers. However, we have many items that retailers and distributors do not find practical to stock, and those are the items that we encourage consumers to order from us.
